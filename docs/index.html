Cherry Battle

A fun logic puzzle. Fill the board with cherries — but never let two touch.

Learn how to playiPhone & iPad · iOS 17+ · Free to play

How to play

  1. Every row, every column and every coloured region holds exactly two cherries.
  2. Two cherries may never touch — not horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally.
  3. Tap a cell to cycle it: empty → a dot (your “no cherry here” note) → a 🍒 cherry → empty again.
  4. Every puzzle has exactly one solution, and most can be cracked by pure logic — no guessing required.

Privacy Policy

Cherry Battle is built to respect your privacy. It does not collect, track, or share any personal information.

  • No data collection. There are no accounts and no sign-in, and the app contains no analytics or advertising. It does not gather your name, email, contacts, location, or device identifiers.
  • Everything stays on your device. Your puzzles, progress, statistics, and settings are stored only on your device. Boards are generated on device, so the app works fully offline and sends nothing to us or anyone else.
  • Purchases. The optional unlock is handled entirely by Apple’s App Store / StoreKit. We never see or store your payment details.
  • No third parties. Cherry Battle includes no third-party SDKs, trackers, or advertising networks.
  • Suitable for all ages. The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
  • Deleting your data. Removing the app deletes all of its data from your device.
