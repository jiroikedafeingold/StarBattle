Cherry Battle

A hand-crafted logic puzzle. Fill the board with cherries — but never let two touch.

Learn how to playiPhone & iPad · iOS 17+

How to play

  1. Every row, every column and every coloured region holds exactly two cherries.
  2. Two cherries may never touch — not horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally.
  3. Tap a cell to cycle it: empty → a dot (your "no cherry here" note) → a 🍒 cherry → empty again.
  4. Every puzzle has exactly one solution, and most can be cracked by pure logic — no guessing required.

Features

🍒

Endless puzzles

A fresh, uniquely-solvable board whenever you want one — generated on device, ready instantly.

🧠

Logic-first

The generator favours puzzles you can solve by deduction alone, so progress always feels fair.

✏️

Highlight mode

Pencil in a whole line of guesses, then "Realize" them onto the board in one satisfying sweep.

🤚

Smart helpers

Placing a cherry auto-dots its neighbours, drag to sweep dots, undo any time, and check your work.

📱

iPhone & iPad

An adaptive board that fills the screen on any device, in portrait or landscape.

🎉

Sweet victory

Solve it and the board erupts in falling cherries and a celebratory rumble of haptics.
