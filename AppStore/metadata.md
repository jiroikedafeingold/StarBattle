## Description (≤4000)
Cherry Battle is a clean, friendly take on Star Battle — the elegant “two-not-touch” logic puzzle.

The rules are simple, the deductions are deep: place two cherries in every row, every column and every coloured region. The catch — no two cherries may ever touch, not even diagonally. From those few rules grows a surprisingly rich puzzle you solve with pure logic.

Every board is generated fresh on your device with exactly one solution, and the game favours puzzles you can crack by reasoning alone — so you never have to guess.

WHY YOU’LL LIKE IT
• Endless puzzles — a new, uniquely-solvable 10×10 board whenever you want one, ready instantly.
• Hints that teach — stuck on a square? A hint places the next move logic forces and explains exactly why, so you learn new tactics instead of just getting the answer.
• Mark mode — pencil in a whole line of candidate guesses, then “Realize” them onto the board in one satisfying sweep.
• Smart helpers — placing a cherry automatically dots its neighbours, drag to sweep dots down a line, and Undo or Redo any move.
• Make it yours — light or dark theme, a quiet timer you can hide, and a playing piece you choose: cherry, star, queen, diamond or heart.
• iPhone & iPad — an adaptive board that fills the screen on any device, in portrait or landscape.
• Sweet victory — solve it and the board erupts in falling cherries and a celebratory rumble of haptics.
• No ads, no accounts, no tracking — and it works completely offline.

Whether you have two minutes in a queue or twenty on the couch, Cherry Battle is a calm, rewarding little brain workout.

Cherry Battle is a cherry-themed take on Star Battle, the classic logic puzzle invented by Hans Eendebak. All credit for the original puzzle design goes to him.
